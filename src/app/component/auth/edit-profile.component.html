Here you can edit your personal informations: email address, username or your password.

To edit your personal information just type the new credentials to the input boxes. If you would like to save the changes, click on the "Save changes" button at the bottom of the page. If you do not click on the button, your changes will not be saved.

To delete your entire profile (and caffs also) click on DELETE PROFILE button. Pay attention! This step is irreversible.

The password must contain at least one upper and lower case character and a number and be 8 long
The confirm password must match the password
