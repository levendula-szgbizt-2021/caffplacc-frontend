Here you can edit your personal information, such as your email address, username or password.

To edit your data, just type the new values to the input boxes. If you would like to save the changes, click the "Save changes" button at the bottom of the page. If you do not click this button, your changes will be lost.

To delete your entire profile (including all your uploaded animations) click the "DELETE PROFILE" button. BEWARE: this operation is irreversible.
